Machine Learning Scientist

Top Data Science is hiring a full-time machine learning scientist, initially for a 6 month project with a possibility of joining the team further on permanently.

Key Responsibilities
  • Dig in (explore and visualize) client data (image, text, time series…) to gain an overall understanding of the problem.
  • Design several machine learning approaches to tackle the problem. Compare and validate those in a scientific manner, having an opinion on what is good-enough for business value.
  • Implement the decided solution as well as the software infrastructure to enable a production-ready output.
  • Transfer, communicate and report the gained knowledge and key findings out of the analysis, internally and externally.

Qualifications
  • “Have fun and enjoy the game” attitude.
  • Background in a quantitative field, e.g., Computer Science, Physics, Mathematics, Statistics, Engineering.
  • Understanding of machine learning concepts and algorithms, both theory and practice.
  • Proficient in Python (R is a plus), familiarity with numpy, scipy, TensorFlow, scikit-learn, pandas, nltk.
  • Hands-on experience with deep learning concepts and architectures.
  • Scientific mindset.
  • Familiarity with Linux command line, scripting and automation.
  • Excellent communications skills, spoken and written, in English.
  • Team player, ability and experience to work in a multicultural setting.
